Ingredients

  • Minced meat (0,5kg)
  • Canned tomatoes (3 cans)
  • Carrots (0,4kg)
  • Cellery (0,3kg)
  • Onions (0,3kg)
  • Olive oil (3 tablespoons)
  • Milk (1 cup)
  • Spaghetti pasta

Steps

  1. Pour olive oil on pan
  2. Fry meat until slightly browned
  3. Remove meat
  4. Add chopped onions and fry until they get slightly golden
  5. Add chopped carrots and cellery
  6. Add chopped tomatoes
  7. Reduce the sauce
  8. Cook over low heat over 3h adding liquid as necessary
  9. 15min before finishing cooking add milk
  10. Boil pasta in salted water till al Dente
  11. Add pasta and a bit of pasta water to sauce
